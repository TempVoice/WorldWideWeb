TempVoice philosophy

Temporary Instead of Permanent!

Voice Channels
Temporary Voices
A Temporary voice channel can be fully customized by the channel owner and will be deleted when everyone leaved.
Thread Channels
Temporary Threads
A Temporary thread can be used as a private chat and will be deleted when the voice gets deleted.
Stage Channels
Temporary Stages
A Temporary stage can be used to create your own events and will deleted when everyone leaved.
Creator Channels
How to create temporary channels?

With Creator Channels. A Creator Channel is like a normal voice channel with the difference that after joining, a new channel is created which is temporary. Temporary threads are linked to voice/stage channels and will also be deleted when they are empty.
