What are temporary voice channels?

Temporary voice channels can be fully customized by the channel owners and will be deleted if they are empty.
All adjustments are saved until the channel is recreated.

What are creator channels?

Creator channel are normal voice channels which are used to create temporary voice channel by joining into them.
You can setup up to 15 creator channels completely for free.
