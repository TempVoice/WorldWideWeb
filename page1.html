/edit

  • name
    Change the name of your voice channel ( Every 10 minutes )
  • limit
    Change the limit of your voice channel  ( 0 - 99 )
  • mode
    Change the mode of your voice channel ( Locked, Unlocked, Hidden, Unhidden )
  • region
    Change the region of your voice channel ( Europe, Amerika... )

/user

  • trust
    Trusted user can always join your voice channel
  • untrust
    Untrusted user are not able to join your locked or hidden voice channel
  • ban
    Banned are not able to view your voice channel
  • unban
    Unbanned user are able to view and join your unlocked or unhidden voice channel
  • invite
    Will invite a user through a dm to your voice channel
  • kick
    Will kick a user from your voice channel
  • lists
    Will show you a list of all trusted and banned user of your voice channel

/config

  • feature
    Deactivate or reactivate a feature
  • limit
    Forces all channels to use this limit
  • name
    Forces all channels to use this name
  • reset
    Will reset the bot config to default
  • show
    Will show you the current config
