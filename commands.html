/voice

A basic command to manage voice channel properties

name

Change the name of your temporary voice channel

limit

Change the user limit of your temporary your voice channel

privacy

Change the privacy of your temporary voice channel

region

Change the region of your temporary voice channel

bitrate

Change the bitrate of your temporary voice channel

claim

Claim a temporary voice channel to gain the ownership of it

trust

Trusted user can always join your temporary voice channel

untrust

Untrusted user are not able to join your locked or hidden temporary voice channel

block

Blocked users are not able to view your temporary voice channel

unblock

Unblocked users are able to view and join your unlocked or unhidden temporary voice channel

invite

Invited users will get an invite through a dm can join your temporary voice channel

kick

Will kick a user from your temporary voice channel

users

Will show you a list of all trusted and banned user of your voice channel

transfer

Transfer the ownership of your temporary voice channel to another user

/config

A admin command to manage how voice channel are created by default

channel

Add a new creator channel or remove an existing one

category

Choose a category that should be used to arrange temporary voice channels

privacy

Choose a default privacy mode for temporary voice channels

name

Choose a default name for temporary voice channels. Try the placeholder {OWNER}, {NUMBER}, {CUSTOM}

limit

Choose a default user limit for temporary voice channels

role

Permit or reject a role for all temporary voice channels

mode

Choose how temporary voice channels should be created

feature

Choose a feature that you want to deactivate or reactivate for your users
