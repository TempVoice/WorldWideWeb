/channel

  • name
    Change the name of your temporary voice channel ¹
  • limit
    Change the user limit of your temporary your voice channel
  • mode
    Change the mode of your temporary voice channel
  • region
    Change the region of your temporary voice channel
  • claim
    Claim a temporary voice channel to gain the ownership of it
  • transfer
    Transfer the ownership of your temporary voice channel to another user
  • reset
    Reset the config of your temporary voice channel

/user

  • trust
    Trusted user can always join your temporary voice channel
  • untrust
    Untrusted user are not able to join your locked or hidden temporary voice channel
  • ban
    Banned users are not able to view your temporary voice channel
  • unban
    Unbanned users are able to view and join your unlocked or unhidden temporary voice channel
  • invite
    Invited users will get an invite through a dm can join your temporary voice channel
  • kick
    Will kick a user from your temporary voice channel'
  • lists
    Will show you a list of all trusted and banned user of your voice channel

/config

  • setup
    Setup the bot to create all required channel
  • command
    Deactivate or reactivate a command for your users
  • limit
    Forces all temporary voice channels to use a specific user limit
  • name
    Forces all temporary voice channels to use a specific name ²
  • channel
    Choose a voice channel that should be used to create temporary voice channels
  • category
    Choose a category that should be used to create temporary voice channels
  • whitelist
    Choose a role that should be able to access temporary voice channels
  • blacklist
    Choose a role that should not be able to access temporary voice channels
  • mode
    Choose how temporary voice channels should be created
  • show
    Will show you the current config
  • reset
    Reset the config of the bot
